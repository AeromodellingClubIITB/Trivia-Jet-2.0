Trivia-Jet 2.0

We are pleased to announce and invite you to TriviaJet 2.0, an online aviation quiz. The quiz will consist of general questions related to space and aviation, nothing specifically related to Aerospace courses. So, we welcome everyone to join us and have fun with the interesting quiz rounds and questions! The TriviaJet quiz consists of two levels, the Prelims and the Finals. Prelims will be a short MCQ based quiz, wherein you can compete with everyone to stand out and get a chance to enter the labyrinth of questions we have created for you.
